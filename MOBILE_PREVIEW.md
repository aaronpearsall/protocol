## Option 1: Expo Go App (Recommended - On Your Phone)

1. **Install Expo Go** on your phone:
   - **iPhone**: [Download from App Store](https://apps.apple.com/app/expo-go/id982107779)
   - **Android**: [Download from Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=host.exp.exponent)

2. **Scan the QR Code**:
   - Look in your terminal for a QR code
   - **iPhone**: Open Camera app, point at QR code
   - **Android**: Open Expo Go app, tap "Scan QR code"

3. **The app will load on your phone!**

## Option 2: iOS Simulator (Mac only)

1. Make sure you have Xcode installed
2. In the terminal where Expo is running, press **`i`**
3. iOS Simulator will open automatically

## Option 3: Android Emulator

1. Make sure you have Android Studio installed and an emulator set up
2. In the terminal where Expo is running, press **`a`**
3. Android Emulator will open

## Option 4: Web Browser (Limited)

1. In the terminal where Expo is running, press **`w`**
2. Opens in browser at http://localhost:8081
3. Note: Some mobile features may not work perfectly in web

## Troubleshooting

**Can't see QR code?**
- Check your terminal output
- Make sure Expo started successfully
- Try: `npx expo start --clear`

**App won't load?**
- Make sure your phone and computer are on the same WiFi
- Check firewall settings
- Try restarting Expo
